Transfer your Optum HSA to Fidelity HSA now!

If you enrolled in either the HSA2600 or HSA3200 medical plan, your new Fidelity HSA account is now ready for you to consolidate your HSA funds.

From January 7 through 25, to request a funds transfer from your Optum HSA, go to myHR North America, click on the Fidelity 401k icon (or visit www.401k.com), and follow the easy instructions. During this time period, CNH Industrial pays all transfer fees involved.

Transferring your HSA balance from Optum helps you manage your account in one place. And, For HSA accounts with a balance over $5,000, the $24 annual administration feel is waived. As part of the transfer process, your Optum funds will be unavailable from February 6 through 16. The transferred funds will become available in your Fidelity HSA account the week of February 17.

Note: If you do not wish to transfer your current Optum account balance during this time period, no action is required. Your account with Optum will remain open and you can continue to use your debit card until the account balance is exhausted. The monthly administration fee of $3.75 will be waived for accounts with an average daily balance of over $2,500.

If you are unable to find what you need on myHR North America, contact CNH Industrial HR Employee Services at 800-561-0877 or nacnhbenefitservices@cnhind.com.
